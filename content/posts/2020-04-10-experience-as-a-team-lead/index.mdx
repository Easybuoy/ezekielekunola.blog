November 2019 I started working as a Technical Team Lead at Lambda School. 6 months down the line I want to write about my experiences and the lessons learned along the way.

The technical team lead position entails managing a cross-functional diverse team remotely from various backgrounds around the world.

During my time as a Technical Team Lead, I worked with a team of 6 with building a project for 2 months. We build a workout tracker application called Trackdrills.
